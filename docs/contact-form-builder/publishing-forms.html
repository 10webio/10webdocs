            For inserting the contact form into a Page or a Post:

            • Open the page or post where you want to post the Contact Form.
            • Check the buttons in your editor. Find and press the button titled as Insert Contact Form.
            • Select a form from the list and press Insert button.
            • Save the page or the post.

            If you do not use the standard WordPress editor, you can add the shortcode of the product manually:

            Add the shortcode [Contact_Form_Builder id=”N”] (where N is the id of the contact form, e.g. if id=2, the shortcode must be [Contact_Form_Builder id=”N”]. Similar to the first instructions you should add the created shortcode into the pages and posts, where you want the form to appear. The shortcode list can be copied from the Manager section in Form Builder > Manager.
