                Introducing Photo Gallery Plugin

                Photo Gallery plugin is a feature-rich, yet easy-to-use WordPress tool, which lets you add mobile-friendly image galleries and gallery groups to your website. Tuned up with a large variety of user-friendly options, Photo Gallery lets you build unlimited galleries and showcase your photos with stunning layouts.

                The plugin is designed to be used by anyone from WordPress beginners to developers. With just a few simple steps, you are able to build and publish galleries, edit and manage images, apply tags and more. Whether you own a photography website, a travel blog, or just wish to share your photos, Photo Gallery is the greatest option for you.

                Photo Gallery plugin comes with 7 gallery layouts:

                • Thumbnails,
                • Masonry,
                • Mosaic,
                • Slideshow,
                • Image Browser,
                • Blog Style,
                • Carousel,

                as well as 3 gallery group layouts:

                • Compact,
                • Masonry,
                • Extended.

                With the help of these options, you are able to create and publish grid galleries with various structures, slideshows, image carousels and more. Besides these layouts, Photo Gallery plugin lets you place your images into any widget areas with the following widgets:

                • WordPress Standard Tag Cloud,
                • Dynamic Photo Gallery Tags Cloud,
                • Photo Gallery Slideshow,
                • Photo Gallery widget.

                Allow users to view full-size images by displaying them with the responsive lightbox of Photo Gallery plugin. Along with the large images, it provides a beautiful filmstrip, a number of control buttons, as well as social sharing tools. By applying one of the 15 animation effects, you will provide amazing navigation through images in the lightbox.

                Bulk embed Instagram and Facebook photos into your galleries, or include videos and images from the following platforms by simply inputting media URLs:

                • YouTube,
                • Vimeo,
                • Instagram,
                • Flickr,
                • Dailymotion.

                Also, you can add an audio track to your slideshow galleries and let users enjoy music while looking through the images.

                In this user guide, we will discuss each and every option, layout and feature of Photo Gallery plugin, helping you create galleries, gallery groups and publish them with the most suitable and unique appearance.
