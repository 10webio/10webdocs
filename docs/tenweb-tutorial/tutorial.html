10Web Tutorial

General Description

10Web Dashboard is the ultimate tool for managing your 10Web plugins, themes, add-ons, downloading or restoring backups, and much more. It lets you install and update several products with just a few clicks. The Dashboard was created to help you save your time and avoid any possible technical difficulties.

Visit 10Web Dashboard and login with your 10Web account. You will access the toolbox which lets you add websites, where you use 10Web products, check and modify your account information and configure 10Web products.

User Settings

After signing in to the Dashboard, press the button with 10Web logo to expand the left menu. Press the gear button to access User Settings, which are separated to the following three sections:

  • Account Settings
  • Subscription Plan
  • Billing

Account Settings

This toolbox lets you edit the main information you have provided when you signed up on 10Web to purchase a product. You can modify the following data easily:

  • Your Avatar
  • Name
  • E-mail address
  • Phone Number
  • Time zone
  • Your account password

After you press Reset Password button, a message will be sent to your email address which will let you change the password you have set initially.

Note, that the avatar of your account is pulled from Gravatar.com. Therefore, you will need to access your Gravatar account to change the picture. It will be updated on 10Web automatically.

Furthermore, you can provide and later edit your business information, VAT for your area (for EU only), website and your local address.

Make sure to press Save after making changes among Account Settings section.

Subscription Plan

Subscription Plan section of User Settings toolbox allows you to review and update the subscription plan you have for your 10Web products, as well as check which Payment Method has been used.

Easily activate the canceled subscription or cancel the ongoing plan. You can re-activate the plan anytime you wish. Also, you can check the information of all payment methods you added by pressing View All Billing Information button. You will access Billing section of User Settings toolbox.

Billing

In case you have multiple payment options, you can select default payment method anytime. It will be used for your next 10Web payment.

At the bottom of this page, you will see the Payment History from your account. It includes the amount that has been paid, payment date and status. In case you need to get an invoice for your 10Web payment, click Download button under Invoice column.

Managing Websites

To add a new website to 10Web Dashboard, expand the left menu by clicking on 10Web logo, then press Add Website button.

Installing 10Web Manager

This page includes an introduction video of 10Web Manager plugin, as well as a few useful links. You need to download and install this plugin to your WordPress website.

Install 10Web Manager plugin to your website the following way:

  1. Access Plugins page from WordPress administrative panel of your website.
  2. Press Add New, then Browse the downloaded .zip file of 10Web Manager.
  3. Click Install Now. After installation is finished, press Activate.

Now you need to sign in to 10Web Manager. Navigate to 10Web Manager page from the left menu of your WordPress dashboard. Then log in with the username and password of your 10Web account.

After making these changes, your website will automatically appear on 10Web Dashboard.

Websites

Navigate to Websites page from 10Web Dashboard to access the list of your websites with 10Web Manager plugin. These websites can have 2 modes:

  • Active: when 10Web Manager is installed and activated on your WordPress website.
  • Inactive: when 10Web Manager was deactivated or deleted from your WordPress website.

Hover over the site you wish to modify or visit, you will see the following links:

  • Manage: press on this button to navigate to products management page of 10Web Dashboard.
  • Site Admin: this link will redirect you to WordPress administrative dashboard of the site, and log you in with a one-time access token.

On the right side of Websites page, you will receive notifications about product installations, updates, account changes, and more. Press OK to close the notification bar.

Managing Products

From Websites page, press Manage button of the site you wish to change. You will access the the management page of 10Web products.

In case you haven’t installed any plugins, click on the Plugins tab, then press Add Plugins button. Install the plugins you wish to use. You can click on More Info button to find information about the product.

Perform the same actions for Add-ons, Themes and Backups sections. When Backup service is enabled for your site, you are able to download and restore backups, check their logs and review backup jobs from Backups tab.

Image Optimizer service of 10Web lets you optimize the pictures of your website. Statistics and other information from Image Optimizer is shown under Image Optimizer tab of this page. You can quickly review the data and receive comprehensive information about your website optimization.

In case you already have installed products, you are able to:

  • activate,
  • deactivate,
  • update,
  • delete

them all or some at once using Bulk Actions. Furthermore, you can search them and filter by active or inactive products, as well as by those which have an available update.
