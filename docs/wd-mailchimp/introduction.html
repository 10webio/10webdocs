                Introduction

                Introduction to MailChimp WordPress plugin.

                MailChimp WD is a functional plugin for integrating MailChimp forms to your WordPress site. It lets users to subscribe to your MailChimp lists, and furthermore, you can have additional form builder functionality with Custom Fields of the plugin.

                This WordPress plugin has many wonderful and useful options, which we will describe in this guide. However, let’s firstly get you started on configuring general settings and integrating your first subscription form to your WordPress site.
